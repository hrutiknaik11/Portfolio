Smart Water Level Detection

● The Water Detection System enables continuous water level monitoring in a tank using an ultrasonic sensor, with the added capability of controlling water flow remotely. It leverages radio waves and Bluetooth technology for seamless data transmission to a central Arduino unit and the user's smartphone, facilitating real-time monitoring and efficient water management.
● The system incorporates essential components such as Arduino microcontroller units, an ultrasonic sensor for precise water level measurements, radio wave transmitter and receiver modules for data transmission, a Bluetooth module for smartphone connectivity, a relay for motor control, and a power supply unit. These components work together to ensure accurate data collection and efficient control of the water pump or motor.
● Users can conveniently monitor the water levels and manage the water pump or motor remotely through a dedicated mobile app. The system's threshold-based control mechanism automatically activates the relay to regulate the water pump or motor when the water level exceeds a predefined threshold, facilitating effective water management and conservation.
